Bio

Shijie is a first-year Ph.D. student at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://en.sjtu.edu.cn/), advised by Prof. [Pengfei Liu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=oIz_CYEAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao). Prior to that, He received the B.Eng. degree in Intelligence Science from [Fudan University](https://www.fudan.edu.cn/en/) in 2024.
